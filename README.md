# HanRia Eco Tech Private Limited
### Turning Nature’s Waste into Tomorrow’s Solutions

At **HanRia Eco Tech Private Limited**, we believe that nature holds the key to sustainable innovation. By converting **agricultural waste, plant fibers, and aquatic biomass** into advanced eco-friendly materials, we create high-performance products for **industries, healthcare, construction, and consumer applications**.

Our work combines **green material science, natural fiber composite technology, and sustainable engineering** to develop products that are **environmentally responsible, cost-effective, and scalable for modern industries**.

With a strong commitment to **sustainability and circular economy principles**, HanRia Eco Tech transforms underutilized natural resources into **innovative solutions for a greener future**.

Development of high-performance composite materials using plant-based fibers such as **banana fiber, coconut fiber, and agricultural residues** to replace conventional synthetic materials.
